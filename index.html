About Sandwich Fckrs

Sandwich Fckrs is a new concept from a group of friends determined to bring their ridiculous ideas to life through food and events. The vision for Sandwich Fckrs was birthed from an irreverence for food trends, and a desire to create memorable edible experiences for your mouth and soul. Inspired by their diverse ethnic roots and nostalgia for the flavors of the Midwest and the East coast, Kate, Alex, and AJ’s goal is to simply give you great food in a friendly atmosphere you can’t get enough of.


FAQ


So you make sandwiches?

It’s complicated. Yes, we can make sandwiches. No, it’s not our focus. Our focus is on flavor that makes your heart sing and your mouth water. Not just sandwiches, just fucking awesome.

Who’s the Chef?

Co-owner and Executive Chef Alex Abdel-Monem has years of experience in fine dining from New York to Miami and now Los Angeles. He established his L.A. street cred when he put Sonny’s Hideaway on the map for food in Highland Park, and continues to wow the masses in West Hollywood as the Chef de Cuisine at Laurel Hardware. In addition, sous chefs, Kate and AJ, are overly opinionated foodies with well-traveled palates, leaving you in the best of hands when it comes to flavor.
